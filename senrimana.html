learn more about thebelovedmoon

thebelovedmoon -- aka Aga-chuu (阿賀忠) -- started his journey as a VTuber on 5 Jul 2020, which is notably during the 2022 Senri Mana event. he has represented as the character throughout July before he became well-known as Mother Agatha (from Horror Shop Games' Mother) before becoming Aga-chuu -- which was coined by a fellow VTuber named Hina Oujo.

he is the one behind many projects, most notably GourmetDayLife (that gave rise to Astraea Centrale), ReactSquad, and successful projects such as #MamaNyoSquad and VTresMarias -- to which he represented as the First Maria on the latter.

learn more about Shouta Aoi

Shouta Aoi (蒼井翔太) -- otherwise known as SHOWTA -- is a prolific singer and actor that has earned accolades as one of the male actors that represent female characters, either on anime or everywhere else. he became well-known from his theatrical debut as Hanajima Saki from Fruits Basket in 2009.

he was later famous in the anime and JRPG community with his voice acting skills that can match any female seiyuus of his time. he is most notable for his role as Senri Mana (aka Kaiser Insight) from Princess Connect! Re:Dive, in which he was featured in some of its live programs many times.
